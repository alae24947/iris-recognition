Security First

Your money is protected by biometric intelligence

NoorBank uses a multi-layer security architecture including iris recognition, real-time monitoring, and encrypted communications to keep every transaction safe.

👁 Iris Authentication

For transactions above 5,000,000 DZD, we require iris image verification. Upload your iris photo — our system matches it against your registered biometric before authorizing. Failed attempts trigger an immediate email alert.

✓ ISO 30107 Compliant

🔒 End-to-End Encryption

All data transmitted to and from NoorBank is encrypted using TLS 1.3 with AES-256. Your personal data and financial details are never stored in plaintext.

✓ Bank-Grade Encryption

📧 Email Verification

Account creation requires email verification. Every login and sensitive action triggers an email notification to your registered address instantly.

✓ Real-time Alerts

🕵 Login Monitoring

We track every login attempt — time, location, and device. Access your full login history from your dashboard and report any suspicious activity immediately.

✓ 24/7 Monitoring

🛡 Fraud Detection

Machine learning models analyze transaction patterns in real-time and flag anomalies for review. Unusual activity automatically triggers security checks.

✓ AI-Powered

✅ Age Verification

Only adults aged 18 and above may open a NoorBank account. Date of birth is verified at registration and stored securely on our encrypted servers.

✓ Regulatory Compliant
Our Story

Built in Algeria, for Algerians

NoorBank was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: give every Algerian access to a modern, transparent, and fully digital banking experience — without the queues, paperwork, or complexity of traditional banking.

We believe that financial tools should be accessible, secure, and easy to use. That's why we built NoorBank from the ground up with biometric security, real-time notifications, and a zero-fee domestic transfer policy.
